-------------------------------------------------
Current Leaders of Indian Army
--------------------------------------------------

Chief of the Army Staff

General Manoj Pande, PVSM, AVSM, VSM, ADC an alumnus of the National Defence Academy, was commissioned in December 1982 in the Corps of Engineers (The Bombay Sappers). The General Officer has commanded an Engineer Regiment during Operation PARAKRAM in the sensitive Pallanwala Sector of Jammu and Kashmir, along the Line of Control. The General Officer is a graduate of Staff College, Camberley (United Kingdom) and attended the Higher Command (HC) and National Defence College (NDC) Courses.

Vice Chief of the Army Staff

Lieutenant General MV Suchindra Kumar, Ati Vishisht Seva Medal, Yudh Seva Medal**, Vishisht Seva Medal assumed the appointment of Vice Chief of The Army Staff, Integrated Headquarters of Ministry of Defence (Army) on 01 Mar 2023.The General Officer is an alumnus of National Defence Academy, Khadakwasla. He was commissioned into 1 ASSAM REGIMENT in June 1985. He commanded 59 Rashtriya Rifles Battalion (ASSAM), an Infantry Brigade and an Infantry Division on the Line of Control. He has also commanded the highly active White Knight Corps. He has held the appointments of Additional Director General Military Intelligence, Director General Military Intelligence and Deputy Chief of Army Staff (Strategy) at Integrated Headquarters of Ministry of Defence (Army) prior to taking over his current appointment. The Gen offr had the privilege of assuming the mantle of the Col of the Regt of Assam Regt and Arunachal Scouts on 01 Mar 2021.
